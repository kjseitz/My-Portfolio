Community

Family Resource Center

Last spring, the Presidential Fellows went to the Family Resource Center to learn about their organization and to assist them with putting together some send home items. We colored paperbags that would be used to hold food that the kids could take home. We also sorted through their books and created piles that had one of each book to send home with the kids. Although it was very simple, it still saved the staff time.

Cards for Seniors

A friend of mine and I are apart of a scholarship group called Give Something Back, so we decided to have a Give Back Gives Back event with the other scholarship students at Pittsburg State University. The same friend of mine also works at a senior assisted living facility, so we made holiday cards for the residents living there. We also did this in February for Valentine's Day. After making the cards, we took them to the facility and handed them out to the residents.

This event was really sweet and I was really happy to do this. I remember visiting my great-grandma when she was living in an assisted living facility and how much it meant to her.
