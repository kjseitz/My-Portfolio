About Me

My name is Kaia Seitz and I am senior at Pittsburg State University. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics and Computing. I am passionate about learning and love to get all the knowledge I can. I am the Vice President of the Computer Science Club and enjoy getting to share and learn about things we have all come across in the field. I try to leave a positive impact on those around me and influence them to become the best people they can be.

The part of my life that has had the most influence on me and who I am is being the oldest sibling of six kids. There were two years that I was an only child and that was a time I will not remember because now I have five amazing siblings. They are the reason I do what I do. As the oldest, I am the role model. They all look up to me and watch every single thing that I do. I have known this for a long time and its shaped my life and its made me want to be a better person for them.


One Word


I chose the word persistent as my one word because that's what I am. Throughout my life I have been put in tough situations and I have always been able to get through them. I get pushed down and I still get back up every time. I am persistent because I keep pushing through even when things are rough.
